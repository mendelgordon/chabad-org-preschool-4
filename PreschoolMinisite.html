Welcome to School!

Chabad Preschool offers an early childhood learning experience for 18 month olds through Pre-Kindergarten as well as Mom and Me classes for toddlers. Our core program is from 9am to 12:30pm with options for extended afternoon as well as full day. Our program places the highest priority on ensuring that every student is well prepared for Kindergarten. In addition, the children explores their Jewish heritage through singing and dancing, painting and creating, holiday cooking, story telling and lots of imaginative play. Each class enjoys all of our weekly specials; Drama, Yoga, Hebrew, Dance, Karate and Shabbat sing.
